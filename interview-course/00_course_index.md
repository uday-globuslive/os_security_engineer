# OS Security Engineer Complete Course (Beginner to Interview-Ready)

## Who this is for
- You are starting from little or no prior knowledge.
- You want to clear Senior OS Security Engineer interviews focused on CVE remediation and container image hardening.

## How to use this course
- Follow modules in order from 01 to 18.
- Do every lab and write your own notes after each module.
- Practice interview answers after Module 10.

## Course Outcomes
By the end, you will be able to:
- Explain Linux, containers, and vulnerability concepts from first principles.
- Remediate CVEs in container images using practical workflows.
- Harden images and reduce attack surface with measurable improvements.
- Build CI/CD security gates with SBOM and signing controls.
- Answer deep technical interview questions with confidence.

## Completion Criteria
- Complete all module checklists.
- Finish all labs with documented outputs.
- Practice at least 100 interview questions with timed responses.
- Produce 5 STAR stories from your own project experience.
- Complete at least 2 capstone projects with measurable outcomes.
- Practice at least 120 commands from the command handbook.
- Complete the 7-day final revision sprint before interviews.
- Complete at least 30 of the 50 debugging labs.
- Practice 10 company-style adapted answers before interviews.
