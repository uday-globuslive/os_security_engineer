# Module 01: Learning Method (How to Actually Clear the Interview)

## Why this module matters
Many people read content passively and forget it. This module helps you retain and apply everything.

## The Learning Loop
Use this 4-step loop for each module:
1. Learn: read the lesson and examples.
2. Apply: run commands and do mini exercises.
3. Explain: summarize the concept in your own words.
4. Defend: answer one interview question on that concept.

## Evidence-Based Study Rules
- Active recall: close the file and try to explain the concept from memory.
- Spaced repetition: revisit weak topics after 1 day, 3 days, and 7 days.
- Deliberate practice: focus extra time on mistakes, not on strengths.

## Weekly Study Rhythm
- 5 days learning + labs.
- 1 day revision and mock interview.
- 1 day rest or lightweight recap.

## Interview Answer Quality Standard
Every answer should include:
- Context.
- Technical action.
- Validation.
- Outcome with numbers.

## Checkpoint
Before moving to next module, confirm:
- You can define 10 core terms without notes.
- You can explain one real-world example.
- You can answer one interview question in under 90 seconds.
