## Top 15 Must-Remember Points
1. CVSS is a starting point, not final priority.
2. Exposure and exploitability drive urgency.
3. Base digest freshness matters.
4. Rebuild and rescan are mandatory.
5. Non-root is baseline, not optional.
6. Multi-stage builds reduce risk.
7. Remove unnecessary runtime binaries.
8. Scanner differences are normal; investigate critical mismatches.
9. Exceptions must be temporary and owned.
10. SBOM improves impact analysis speed.
11. Signing enforces artifact trust.
12. Canary rollout reduces patch risk.
13. Runtime policy completes image security.
14. Metrics prove impact.
15. Communication quality influences hiring decisions.
