# Tell Me About Yourself - Tailored for Senior OS Security Engineer

## How to use this file
- Use the 45-second version for recruiter or first-round intros.
- Use the 90-second version for technical interview openings.
- Use the 2-minute version when the interviewer wants more detail.
- Keep your tone natural and conversational, not memorized.

## What this JD is looking for
This role is centered on:
- Linux and container security depth.
- Hands-on CVE remediation, not just scanning.
- Image hardening and attack surface reduction.
- Automation of patching and rebuild pipelines.
- SBOM, supply chain integrity, and CI/CD integration.

Your strongest matching resume points are:
- 13+ years in DevOps, cloud, automation, and platform engineering.
- Strong AKS/EKS and Kubernetes background.
- Trivy, SBOM, Jenkins, Nexus, and DevSecOps implementation at Infosys.
- Docker, image scanning, multi-stage builds, and container security practices.
- Linux, Ansible, Terraform, and cloud automation at scale.

## What to avoid saying
- Do not say you only worked on scanning if the role expects remediation.
- Do not over-focus on broad multi-cloud topics in the first answer.
- Do not list too many tools without showing outcomes.
- Do not make it sound like a generic DevOps introduction.

After this answer, expect these follow-ups:
1. Tell me about a critical CVE you personally remediated.
2. How do you reduce image attack surface?
3. Why do HIGH CVEs remain after patching?
4. What is your experience with SBOMs and supply chain security?
5. How have you integrated security into CI/CD?
