
Job Title:  Senior OS Security Engineer – CVE Remediation & Image Hardening
Location: Remote
What You’ll Do
Own end-to-end CVE remediation across Linux-based container images
Analyze vulnerabilities across OS packages, libraries, and dependencies
Patch, rebuild, and validate hardened container images
Reduce attack surface by removing unnecessary packages and components
Build and scale automated pipelines for continuous patching and image rebuilding
Generate and maintain SBOMs and ensure supply chain integrity
Partner with platform and security teams to integrate into CI/CD workflows
Continuously improve image performance, size, and security posture
What You Bring
5+ years in Linux systems, security, or platform engineering
Deep understanding of Linux distributions (Debian, Alpine, RHEL, etc.)
Strong experience with containers and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
Hands-on experience remediating CVEs (not just scanning)
Familiarity with vulnerability tools (Grype, Trivy)
Experience with package managers (apt, yum, apk)
Strong scripting or programming skills (Python, Bash, or Go)
Solid understanding of container image layering and optimization
Nice to Have
Experience building minimal/distroless images
Familiarity with SBOM tools and formats (SPDX, CycloneDX)
Experience with signing and provenance (cosign, sigstore)
Knowledge of supply chain security frameworks (SLSA)
Background in security or high-scale SaaS environments
What Success Looks Like
Deliver container images with near-zero CVEs
Build scalable, automated remediation pipelines
Significantly reduce image size and attack surface
Enable customers to deploy secure containers without friction
